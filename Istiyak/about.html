L.D. College Of Engineering is situated in Ahmedabad in Gujarat state of India. Established in 1948, it is accredited from UGC, NBA, AICTE and it is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University. LDCE, Ahmedabad offers 35 courses across 2 streams namely Engineering, IT and across 4 degrees like B.Tech, M.Tech, PGD, MCA. LDCE campus is spread over 45220 Acres. Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Math Lab, Boys Hostel, Canteen, Computer Lab, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room, Music, Placement, Sports are also there.
